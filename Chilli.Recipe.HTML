Chili Recipe

This is a special Recipe for someone very speacial to me!

Ingredients you need

  • 1 Tablespoon of Olive Oil
  • 2 Bell Peppers
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic
  • 2 Big Tomatoes or about 2 handfuls of little Tomatoes
  • Ground Beef
  • 1 Jar of Tomato Sauce
  • Half a Jar of Water
  • Table Spoon Tomato Paste
  • 1 Teaspoon of Ketchup
  • 1 Can of Corn
  • 1 Can of Red Beans

Seasonings

  • Garlic Salt
  • Smoked Paprika Powder
  • Curry Powder
  • Chili Powder
  • Oregano

Instructions

  1. Cut your Garlic Cloves into small pieces and put it in a Pan with some olive oil. Do not turn on the heat yet.
  2. Cut up your peppers and tomatoes. Turn on the stove when you are almost done.
  3. Let your Garlic brown a little and add your Ground Beef.
  4. Once the Ground Beef is done add your Peppers and the Tomatoes.
  5. Now add your Tomato Paste, Ketchup and the Tomato Sauce. Fill the Tomato Sauce Jar halfway up and also add the Water.
  6. Season your Chili with all the seasonings.
  7. Put the Lid on and let your Chili cook for about 15 minutes on medium heat.
  8. Add the Corn and the Red Beans. Wait 2-3 Minutes before continuing.
  9. Taste it and add more seasonings if needed.

YAY you made your Chili :)
